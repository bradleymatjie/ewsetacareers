About us

Who we are

As one of South Africa’s 21 SETAs, the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority (EWSETA) was established in terms of Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998, as amended and reports to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology. This portal aims to educate website visitors about the various careers in the Energy & Water sector in South Africa.

What We Do

Our Sectors

SA's water Sector

South Africa's water sector is dedicated to providing access to clean and safe water for all citizens.

SA'S Energy Sector

South Africa's energy sector is committed to providing reliable and affordable energy to support the country's economic growth and development.

Why We Built This Website

We are committed to working with stakeholders across the country, including government, businesses, and communities, to find solutions to the challenges facing our water and energy systems. By working together, we can ensure a brighter and more sustainable future for South Africa.

We are proud of the progress we have made in providing access to clean water and energy for all South Africans, and we will continue to work towards a future where every person has access to these essential resources.

Psychometric Tests

Are you a high school student struggling to decide which career path to take? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming and confusing. That’s where psychometric testing comes in.

By taking a psychometric test, you can identify your strengths, weaknesses, interests, and personality traits, which can help you determine which careers are best suited for you. These tests provide valuable insights into your skills and abilities, giving you the knowledge and confidence you need to make informed decisions about your future.
